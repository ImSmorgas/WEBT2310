About Us

“Taking mens grooming from errand to experience.”

Take some time for you… In this fast paced world we live in it is more vital than ever to pause. Self care is an underrated practice and unlike women, men have very little self care options in the grooming industry. 1839 Barber Company is here to show the taboo of pampering men is over. Here is your chance to experience something new and refreshing. 1839 Barber Company is a hybrid barbershop utilizing old school services and modernizing them. Whether your hair style is evolving, you need cleaned up for a special occasion or want to try a relaxing experience, were here to service you. Below you will find our booking options. Come on in, grab a local beer or spirit and let’s get you looking your best, but feeling even better.

- Ashleigh, Owner & Founder of 1839 Barber Co.
